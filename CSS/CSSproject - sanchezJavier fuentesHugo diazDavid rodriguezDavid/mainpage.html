(2021-11-26) Human is changing the sky: the limits between the layers are varying.

Greenhouse gases have caused the troposphere to expand and the stratosphere to contract. Alterations can affect the climate

The sky is changing. For several decades, the boundaries between the layers of the atmosphere have been changing. The height of the two layers closest to the earth, the troposphere and the stratosphere, is being modified by greenhouse gases (GHGs).

(2021-11-17) SPAIN IS WORKING ON A CLIMATIC LAW

Spanish government is taking some measures to promote self-consumption energy, but most of the actions aimed at transport are missing.

From past march, Spain has a Climatic Change and Energetic Transition Law that aims to help the country to reach climatic neutrality (greenhouse gases that can be absorbed by natural sinks, such as oceans and forests) before 2050.
